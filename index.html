Video Music Sync

If you have a video whose background music is drowned out by voices or ambient noise, and a separate clean audio file of that same music, this tool automatically finds the right offset and mixes them together. Everything runs locally in your phone's browser using standard Web Audio / MediaRecorder APIs — your video and audio files are never uploaded anywhere, and there's no large engine to download first.
