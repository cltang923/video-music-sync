Video Music Sync

If you have a video whose background music is drowned out by voices or ambient noise, and a separate clean audio file of that same music, this tool automatically finds the right offset and mixes them together. Everything runs locally in your phone's browser — your video and audio files are never uploaded anywhere. The first run needs network access to download the processing engine (~30MB); it's reused for the rest of that browser tab session.
